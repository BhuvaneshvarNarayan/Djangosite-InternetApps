About Our Club

The University Club was founded in 1990 with the mission to provide a platform for students to engage in extracurricular activities that complement their academic journey.

Over the years, we have achieved numerous milestones and have grown into a diverse community of students and alumni.

  1. 1990 - Club founded
  2. 1995 - First annual seminar held
  3. 2000 - Expanded to include community service projects
  4. 2010 - Launched leadership development program
  5. 2020 - Celebrated 30th anniversary with a major event
